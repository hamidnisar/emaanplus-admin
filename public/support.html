Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen tap Forgot password? and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link in your inbox within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

Why can't I take another quiz yet?

Each quiz has a 1-hour cooldown after completion. A countdown timer is shown on the quiz screen. You can watch a rewarded ad to skip the timer or unlock a new quiz immediately.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly inside the app: go to your Profile screen, scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account under the Danger Zone section. Your account, scores, and all personal data will be permanently removed immediately. If you experience any issue with in-app deletion, email us at contact@emaanplus.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and we will delete your data within 7 days.

The Qibla or prayer times seem incorrect. What should I do?

Make sure location permission is granted in your device settings (Settings → Emaan Plus → Location → While Using). Also ensure your device's GPS is enabled. If the issue persists, contact us with your city and country.

I'm not receiving push notifications.

Go to your device Settings → Notifications → Emaan Plus and make sure notifications are enabled. On iOS you may also need to allow notifications when prompted on first launch.

How does the referral system work?

Find your unique referral code in your Profile screen. Share it with friends. When a new user registers with your code, you earn 5 bonus points automatically.

How do I change the app language?

Open the Settings drawer (top-right icon on the home screen) and choose between English and Urdu under the Language section.

I found a bug or want to suggest a feature.

We love feedback! Email us at contact@emaanplus.com with a description of the issue or your idea. Please include your device model and operating system version.
