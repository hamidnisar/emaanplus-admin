Privacy Policy

Emaan Plus – Islamic Quiz & Learning

Last updated: May 7, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Emaan Plus collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information (Email, Google Sign-In, or Sign in with Apple)

When you create an account we collect your name and email address. If you sign in with Google we also receive your profile photo. If you sign in with Apple on iOS, Apple shares your name and email address on first sign-in only; on subsequent sign-ins only an anonymised user identifier is shared.

Quiz & Game Data

Your quiz scores, number of quizzes played, last played time, and referral information are stored to power the leaderboard and your profile.

Location Data

We request approximate or precise location only to calculate the Qibla direction and local prayer times. Location is processed on-device and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Device & Usage Data

We collect a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to send you push notifications. Standard usage data (crash reports, performance metrics) may be collected by Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your score on the leaderboard and rankings
  • Calculate Qibla direction and prayer times based on your location
  • Send push notifications about new content and reminders (you can disable these in your device settings)
  • Process referral codes and reward referrers
  • Improve the app through aggregated, anonymised usage analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements (see Section 4)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

4. Advertising

Emaan Plus displays ads provided by Google AdMob and Meta Audience Network (Facebook). These services may collect certain device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads.

We request non-personalised ads only by default to respect your privacy. You can review each provider's privacy policy below:

  • Google AdMob – Privacy Policy
  • Meta Audience Network – Privacy Policy

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) – authentication, database, cloud messaging, analytics – Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In – optional OAuth sign-in – Privacy Policy
  • Sign in with Apple – optional OAuth sign-in on iOS – Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS – app build and update infrastructure – Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Emaan Plus is designed for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via device settings
  • Revoke location permission at any time via device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email in Section 8.
